# Product Overview — MushroomAI

## Tagline
"Know before you eat."

## What It Is
MushroomAI is a conversational AI web application that identifies mushrooms from photos or text descriptions. Users chat naturally with the AI to learn whether a mushroom is safe to eat, what species it is, and what they need to know before foraging. Built for non-experts, designed for use in the field.

## The Problem
Mushroom misidentification is dangerous — thousands of poisonings annually. Toxic and edible species look alike. Field guides require expert knowledge; forums are too slow. No fast, reliable tool exists for non-experts in the field.

## The Solution
Mobile-first AI chat interface: upload a photo or describe what you see, receive instant species identification with confidence level, clear safety rating (Safe/Caution/Toxic/Unknown), species profile, and look-alike warnings. Natural language follow-up questions supported.

## Target Users
1. Casual foragers — picking wild mushrooms for cooking
2. Hikers & outdoor enthusiasts — encountering mushrooms on trail
3. Mycology hobbyists — learning to identify species
4. Home cooks & foodies — curious about wild and specialty mushrooms

## Core Features (MVP)
1. Photo Identification — upload photo; AI identifies species with confidence level
2. Natural Language Chat — follow-up questions in plain language
3. Safety Rating — Safe / Caution / Toxic / Unknown — never ambiguous
4. Species Profile — scientific name, common name, habitat, season, distinguishing features
5. Look-alike Warnings — flags dangerous similar species
6. Identification History — saved log of past identifications per session

## Brand & Tone
- Trustworthy, clear, safety-first. Knowledgeable but never condescending.
- Earthy, nature-inspired. Deep forest greens, warm ambers, cream whites.
- Never overstate AI confidence. When uncertain, say so and recommend expert verification.

## Strategic Principles
1. Safety first — uncertainty always disclosed; look-alike warnings always shown
2. Non-expert accessible — plain language throughout
3. Speed in the field — results in seconds, mobile-first
4. Transparent AI — confidence scores shown; never a black-box verdict
