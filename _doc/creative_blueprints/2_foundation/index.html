The Problem

Mushrooms kill — and they look alike.

Thousands of poisonings occur every year because toxic and edible species are visually similar. Field guides require expert knowledge; online forums are slow and unreliable. Non-experts have no fast, trustworthy tool they can use in the field.

The fix ↓
The Solution

An AI expert, always in your pocket.

MushroomAI lets anyone photograph or describe a mushroom and receive instant identification — a clear safety rating, species profile, and look-alike warnings — through natural conversation, in seconds.
